How does it work?

You log in using your Resy username and password.
You request a restaurant, party size, and desired time range. We try to get your reservation even if it not available at the moment.

How much does it cost?

We charge $15/reservation successfully booked. If we are unable to book your request, you will not be charged.

Can I cancel my request or reservation?

Yes. You can cancel your request under the "My requests" tab. You can cancel a booked reservation using the Resy app.

When will I know if you booked my request?

At least 48 hours before the date and time of the reservation.
